New Mini SMB

New Mini SMB was a hack made by Nitro Team. It came from a revival of old mods made by team members. It was a full 8-world hack that didn't want to fully new-ify NSMBDS, but to offer a new spin on the style used throughout the base game.
Sadly, the team collapsed and the mod today is discontinued, but a demo still exists.
